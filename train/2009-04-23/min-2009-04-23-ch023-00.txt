23. Problem of profiling, notably on the basis of ethnicity and race, in
counter-terrorism, law enforcement, immigration, customs and border control
(debate)

Report with a proposal for a European Parliament recommendation to the
Council on the problem of profiling, notably on the basis of ethnicity and
race, in counter-terrorism, law enforcement, immigration, customs and
border control [2008/2020(INI)] - Committee on Civil Liberties, Justice and
Home Affairs. Rapporteur: Sarah Ludford (A6-0222/2009)

Sarah Ludford introduced the report.

The following spoke: Lszl Kovcs (Member of the Commission).

The following spoke: Claude Moraes, on behalf of the PSE Group, Carl
Schlyter, on behalf of the Verts/ALE Group, and Emine Bozkurt.

The following spoke: Lszl Kovcs and Sarah Ludford.

The debate closed.

Vote: minutes of 24.4.2009, item 7.6.
