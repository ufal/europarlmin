11. 2007 discharge: EU general budget - Council (debate)

Report on discharge in respect of the implementation of the European Union
general budget for the financial year 2007
Section II - Council [C6-0417/2008 - 2008/2277(DEC)] - Committee on
Budgetary Control. Rapporteur: Sren Bo Sndergaard (A6-0150/2009)

The following spoke: Sren Bo Sndergaard, first to wonder about the
absence of the Council (the President confirmed that the Council had been
invited to the debate) and then to present his report.

The following spoke: Jos Javier Poms Ruiz, on behalf of the PPE-DE Group,
Costas Botopoulos, on behalf of the PSE Group, Kysti Virrankoski, on
behalf of the ALDE Group, Bart Staes, on behalf of the Verts/ALE Group,
Jens Holm, on behalf of the GUE/NGL Group, Nils Lundgren, on behalf of the
IND/DEM Group, Hans-Peter Martin, Non-attached Member, Herbert Bsch and
Paulo Casaca.

The following spoke under the 'catch-the-eye' procedure: Karl von Wogau.

The following spoke: Hans-Peter Martin, to make a personal statement
following the speech by Karl von Wogau, and the latter, to reply to him.

The following spoke under the 'catch-the-eye' procedure: Paul Rbig.

                     IN THE CHAIR: Edward McMILLAN-SCOTT
                               Vice-President

The following spoke: Herbert Bsch (Chair of the CONT Committee), returning
to Karl von Wogau's first speech.

The following spoke under the 'catch-the-eye' procedure: Ingeborg Grle
and Pierre Pribetich.

The following spoke: Sren Bo Sndergaard.

The debate closed.

Vote: minutes of 23.4.2009, item 6.14.
