7. Award of the Sakharov Prize - 20th Anniversary (Formal sitting)

From 12.15 to 12.50, Parliament held a formal sitting on the occasion of
the award of the Sakharov Prize to Hu Jia, in the presence of Elena Bonner,
widow of Andrei Sakharov, and former winners of the Sakharov Prize.
