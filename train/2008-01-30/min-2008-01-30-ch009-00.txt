9. Approval of the minutes of the previous sitting

The minutes of the previous sitting were approved.

                                      
                                      

The following spoke: Nigel Farage, who spoke against the disciplinary
proceedings announced on 14.01.2008 (minutes of 14.01.2008, item 2) and

of the European Union at Parliament on 12.12.2007 (minutes of 12.12.2007,
item 4).
