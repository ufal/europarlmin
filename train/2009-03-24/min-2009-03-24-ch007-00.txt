7. Preparation of the G20 Summit on 2 April 2009 (debate)

Preparation of the G20 Summit on 2 April 2009 - with the participation of
Gordon Brown, Prime Minister of the United Kingdom, Member of the European
Council

The President made a statement introducing the debate on the preparation of
the G20 Summit.

The following spoke: Gordon Brown (Prime Minister of the United Kingdom of
Great Britain and Northern Ireland).

The following spoke: Jos Manuel Barroso (President of the Commission).

The following spoke: Joseph Daul, on behalf of the PPE-DE Group, Martin
Schulz, on behalf of the PSE Group, Graham Watson, on behalf of the ALDE
Group, Brian Crowley, on behalf of the UEN Group, Monica Frassoni, on
behalf of the Verts/ALE Group, Francis Wurtz, on behalf of the GUE/NGL
Group, Nigel Farage, on behalf of the IND/DEM Group, and Daniel Hannan, Non-
attached Member.

The following spoke: Gordon Brown.

The President thanked Gordon Brown for coming to the European Parliament
and wished him much success in the preparation and conclusion of the G20
Summit.

The debate closed.

                      IN THE CHAIR: Alejo VIDAL-QUADRAS
                               Vice-President
