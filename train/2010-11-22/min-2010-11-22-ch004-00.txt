4. Composition of Parliament

The Spanish authorities had given notice of the election to the European
Parliament of Mara Irigoyen Prez to replace Ramn Juregui Atondo.
Parliament took note with effect from 16 November 2010.

Under Rule 3(2), until such time as her credentials had been verified or a
ruling had been given on any dispute, and provided that she had previously
made a written declaration stating that she did not hold any office
incompatible with that of  Member of the European Parliament, Mara
Irigoyen Prez would take her seat in Parliament and on its bodies and
would enjoy all the rights attaching thereto.
