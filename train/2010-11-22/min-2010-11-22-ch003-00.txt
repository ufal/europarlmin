3. Statements by the President

The President made a statement on the occasion of the liberation of the
Burmese opposition leader Aung San Suu Kyi. He pointed out that she had
been awarded the Sakharov Prize in 1990 but had never been able to come in
person to collect her prize. He added that he had invited Aung San Suu Kyi
to attend a forthcoming part-session of the European Parliament.

The President made a statement to mark the 11th UN International Day for
the Elimination of Violence against Women on 25 November 2010.
