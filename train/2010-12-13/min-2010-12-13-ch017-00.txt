17. Immigration liaison officers network ***I (debate)

Report on the proposal for a regulation of the European Parliament and of
the Council amending Council Regulation (EC) No 377/2004 on the creation of
an immigration liaison officers network [COM(2009)0322 - C7-0055/2009-
2009/0098(COD)] - Committee on Civil Liberties, Justice and Home Affairs.
Rapporteur: Agustn Daz de Mera Garca Consuegra (A7-0342/2010)

Agustn Daz de Mera Garca Consuegra introduced the report.

The following spoke: Cecilia Malmstrm (Member of the Commission).

The following spoke: Carlos Coelho, on behalf of the PPE Group, Claude
Moraes, on behalf of the S&D Group, Stanimir Ilchev, on behalf of the ALDE
Group, Franziska Keller, on behalf of the Verts/ALE Group, Rui Tavares, on

The following spoke under the 'catch-the-eye' procedure: Simon Busuttil,
Oreste Rossi, Franz Obermayr, Csand Szegedi, Andrew Henry William Brons
and Krisztina Morvai.

The following spoke: Cecilia Malmstrm and Agustn Daz de Mera Garca
Consuegra.
