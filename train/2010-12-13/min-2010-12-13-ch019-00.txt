19. Extension of the scope of Directive 2003/109/EC to beneficiaries of
international protection ***I (debate)

Report on the proposal for a directive of the European Parliament and of
the Council amending Directive 2003/109/EC to extend its scope to
beneficiaries of international protection [COM(2007)0298 - C6-0196/2007-
2007/0112(COD)] - Committee on Civil Liberties, Justice and Home Affairs.
Rapporteur: Claude Moraes (A7-0347/2010)

Claude Moraes introduced the report.

The following spoke: Cecilia Malmstrm (Member of the Commission).

The following spoke: Mariya Nedelcheva, on behalf of the PPE Group, Cecilia
Wikstrm, on behalf of the ALDE Group, Judith Sargentini, on behalf of the
Verts/ALE Group, Simon Busuttil and Ioan Enciu.

The following spoke under the 'catch-the-eye' procedure: Franz Obermayr.

The following spoke: Cecilia Malmstrm and Claude Moraes.

The debate closed.

Vote: minutes of 14.12.2010, item 9.11.
