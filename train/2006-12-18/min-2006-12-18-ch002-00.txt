2. Approval of Minutes of previous sitting

The Minutes of the previous sitting were approved.

The following spoke:

- Jean-Marie Cavada (Chairman of the LIBE Committee), who thanked all those
involved on 14 December 2006 in the adoption of ten proposals for decisions
on the financing of the Area of Freedom, Security and Justice (Minutes of
14.12.2006, Item 6.11, Minutes of 14.12.2006, Item 6.12, Minutes of
14.12.2006, Item 6.13, Minutes of 14.12.2006, Item 6.15, Minutes of
14.12.2006, Item 6.16, Minutes of 14.12.2006, Item 6.22, Minutes of
14.12.2005, Item 6.23, Minutes of 14.12.2006, Item 6.26, Minutes of
14.12.2006, Item 6.27 and Minutes of 14.12.2006, Item 6.28) and added that
the texts now reflected the wishes of the House;

- Richard Corbett (rapporteur on the amendment to Rule 81 of the Rules of
Procedure, adopted on 14.12.2006 - Minutes of 14.12.2006, Item 6.20), who
asked that the new provisions should be made available in January 2007 (the
President said a new provisional version of the Rules of Procedure would be
distributed for January 2007).
