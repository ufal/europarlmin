11. Question Time (Council)

Parliament considered a number of questions to the Council (B6-0457/2008).

Question 1 (Manuel Medina Ortega): Policy on the supply of agricultural
products.
Jean-Pierre Jouyet (President-in-Office of the Council) answered the
question and supplementaries by Manuel Medina Ortega, Jim Allister and Paul
Rbig.


Question 2 (Marie Panayotopoulos-Cassiotou): European Youth Pact.
Jean-Pierre Jouyet answered the question and supplementaries by Marie
Panayotopoulos-Cassiotou and Jrg Leichtfried.


Question 3 (Robert Evans): EU overseas territorial anomalies.
Jean-Pierre Jouyet answered the question and supplementaries by Robert
Evans, Justas Vincas Paleckis and Avril Doyle.


Question 4 (Avril Doyle): Border Adjustment Measures (BAMs) on less carbon-
efficient imports.
Jean-Pierre Jouyet answered the question and supplementaries by Avril Doyle
and Syed Kamall.


Question 5 (Colm Burke): High-Level Forum on Aid Effectiveness in Accra
(Ghana) in September.
Jean-Pierre Jouyet answered the question and a supplementary by Colm Burke.


Question 6 (Jim Higgins): EU mission in Chad.
Jean-Pierre Jouyet answered the question and supplementaries by Jim
Higgins, Colm Burke and Marian Harkin.

Questions 7 to 12, 14, 16, 17, 19 to 24, and 26 to 34 would receive written
answers (see annex to the Verbatim Report).

Council Question Time closed.
         (The sitting was suspended at 19.05 and resumed at 21.05.)
