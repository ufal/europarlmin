1. Opening of the sitting

The sitting opened at 9.00.

                                      
                                      

The following spoke: Monica Frassoni, who said the Commission had
activated the European Alert System for  radioactive hazards after an

Council should report on the situation that morning. (The President
answered that the Presidency-in-Office of the Council would speak on the
matter) (minutes of 05.06.2008, item 4).
