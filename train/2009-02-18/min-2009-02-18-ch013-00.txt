13. Order of business

The next item was the order of business.
The final draft agenda for the February II sittings (PE 420.290/PDOJ) had
been distributed and a number of changes had been proposed (Rule 132):

Wednesday

Request by the PPE-DE Group to hold over until the next part-session the
debate on Reul report (A6-0035/2009) (item 17 on the final draft agenda)

The following spoke: Herbert Reul, on behalf of the PPE-DE Group, who moved
the request, Hannes Swoboda, on behalf of the PSE Group, who requested that
the vote on the report by Thijs Berman (A6-0036/2009) also be held over
until a later part-session, and Claude Turmes, on behalf of the Verts/ALE
Group.

Parliament accepted the request by the PPE-DE Group.

The request by the PSE Group would be put to the vote the next day.
Thursday

Request by the PPE-DE Group to hold over until the next part-session the
vote on the Evans report (A6-0011/2009) on competition policy in 2006 and
2007.

Request under Rule 118 by the TRAN Committee that the Committee of the
Regions be consulted on the urban mobility action plan.

Request by the LIBE Committee to include among the next day's votes the
final vote on the report by Claudio Fava (A6-0026/2009) (minutes of
04.02.2009, item 7.1).

Parliament accepted those requests.

The order of business was thus established.
