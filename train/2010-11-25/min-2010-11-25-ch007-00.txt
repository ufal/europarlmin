7. Camp Ashraf (written declaration)

Written declaration 0075/2010, submitted by Alejo Vidal-Quadras, Stephen
Hughes, Kristiina Ojuland, Sren Bo Sndergaard and Struan Stevenson on
Camp Ashraf had, by 25 November 2010, been signed by the majority of
Parliament's component Members. Consequently, in accordance with Rule
123(3) and (4), it would be forwarded to its addressees and published in
the Texts Adopted of that sitting (P7_TA(2010)0452), together with the
names of the signatories (see Annex 2).

The following spoke: Struan Stevenson.
