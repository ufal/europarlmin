4. Conclusions of the European Council meeting (debate)

European Council and Commission statements: Conclusions of the European
Council meeting on 16-17 December

Herman Van Rompuy (President of the European Council) and Jos Manuel
Barroso (President of the Commission) made the statements.

The following spoke: Joseph Daul, on behalf of the PPE Group, Stephen
Hughes, on behalf of the S&D Group, Sylvie Goulard, on behalf of the ALDE
Group, Rebecca Harms, on behalf of the Verts/ALE Group, Timothy Kirkhope,
on behalf of the ECR Group, Joe Higgins, on behalf of the GUE/NGL Group,
Nigel Farage, on behalf of the EFD Group, Barry Madlener, Non-attached
Member, Olle Schmidt to put a blue-card question to Nigel Farage, who
replied, Jos Manuel Garca-Margallo y Marfil, Pervenche Bers, Martin
Callanan, Miguel Portas, Mario Borghezio, Ioannis Kasoulides, Anni
Podimata, Ilda Figueiredo and Tunne Kelam.

The following spoke under the 'catch-the-eye' procedure: Jean-Pierre Audy,
Roberto Gualtieri, Ildik Gll-Pelcz, Nikolaos Salavrakos, Liisa
Jaakonsaari and Sen Kelly.

The following spoke: Jos Manuel Barroso and Herman Van Rompuy.

The debate closed.
