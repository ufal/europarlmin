1. Opening of the sitting with the Oldest Member in the Chair (first
sitting of the newly elected Parliament)

The sitting opened at 10.05.

In accordance with the Act concerning the election of representatives of
the European Parliament by direct universal suffrage, the oldest Member
declared open the first sitting of the European Parliament after the
elections.

He made a brief speech, during which he made a point of welcoming the
representatives of the new Member States of the Union.
