3. Statements by the President

On behalf of Parlaiment, the President condemned the murders on 18 April
2007 of three workers at a Christian publishing house in Malatya (Turkey).
He called on the Turkish authorities to do their utmost to bring those
responsible to justice.

                                      
                                      

The President pointed out that Boris Yeltsin, the former President of
Russia, had died that day. The President passed on Parliament's condolences
to the Russian people.
