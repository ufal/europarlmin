6. Formal sitting - Palestinian Authority

From 12.00 to 12.40, a formal sitting of Parliament was held on the
occasion of de the address by Mahmoud Abbas, President of the Palestinian
Authority.

                         IN THE CHAIR: Diana WALLIS
                               Vice-President

The following spoke: Urszula Gacek, calling on Parliament to support the
efforts of the Polish and Pakistani governments to secure the release of a
Polish engineer kidnapped in Pakistan.
