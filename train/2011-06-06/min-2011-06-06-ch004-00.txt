4. Announcements by the President

The President recalled the 22nd anniversary of the student demonstrations
in Tiananmen Square, Beijing, by the authorities of the People's Republic
of China.

He also recalled the 22nd anniversary of the first free parliamentary
elections in Poland, which were won by the democratic opposition.

                                      
                                      

The President announced that an exceptional evacuation exercise would take
place during the current part-session in Strasbourg, with another scheduled
for the June mini-session in Brussels.

                                      
                                      

The President announced that Silvana Koch-Mehrin had submitted her
resignation as Vice-President of the European Parliament with effect from
11 May 2011. Pursuant to Rule 18 of the Rules of Procedure, Parliament
established that the post would be vacant.
