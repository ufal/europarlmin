12. Question Time (Council)

Parliament considered a number of questions to the Council (B6-0018/2007).

Question 1 (Rodi Kratsa-Tsagaropoulou): Political crisis in Turkey and
prospects of accession.
Gnter Gloser (President-in-Office of the Council) answered the question
and supplementaries by Rodi Kratsa-Tsagaropoulou, Reinhard Rack and Andreas
Mlzer.


Question 2 (Manuel Medina Ortega): Frontex activity on the Union's maritime
borders.
Gnter Gloser answered the question and supplementaries by Manuel Medina
Ortega, Simon Busuttil and Marie Anne Isler Bguin.


Question 3 (Marie Panayotopoulos-Cassiotou): Security or freedom for
European citizens at airports.
Gnter Gloser answered the question and supplementaries by Marie
Panayotopoulos-Cassiotou, Robert Evans, Justas Vincas Paleckis and Marie
Panayotopoulos-Cassiotou.


Question 6 (Sarah Ludford): Gay Pride marches.
Gnter Gloser answered the question and a supplementary by Sarah Ludford.


Question 7 (Bernd Posselt): Human rights in Chechnya.
Gnter Gloser answered the question and a supplementary by Bernd Posselt.


Question 8 (Philip Bushill-Matthews): Tariffs on light bulbs.
Gnter Gloser answered the question and a supplementary by Philip Bushill-
Matthews.


Question 9 (Marie Anne Isler Bguin): Agreement on facilitation of the visa
system for nationals of the Russian Federation.
Gnter Gloser answered the question and supplementaries by Marie Anne Isler
Bguin and Justas Vincas Paleckis.

Questions which had not been answered for lack of time would receive
written answers (see annex to the Verbatim Report of Proceedings).

Council Question Time closed.
         (The sitting was suspended at 19.05 and resumed at 21.00.)
