1. Opening of sitting

The sitting opened at 10.05.

The following spoke: Hans-Gert Pttering, on behalf of the PPE-DE Group,
who condemned comments made by the Iranian President Mahmoud Ahmadinejad,
in which he had called for Israel to be wiped off the map. Mr Poettering
asked for the matter to be placed on the agenda of the next part-session.
Hannes Swoboda, on behalf of the PSE Group, and Silvana Koch-Mehrin, on
behalf of the ALDE Group, both supported Hans-Gert Pttering's request (the
President replied that the matter would be referred to the Conference of
Presidents).
