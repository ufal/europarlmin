21. The transposition, implementation and enforcement of Directives
2005/29/EC and 2006/114/EC (short presentation)

Report: on the transposition, implementation and enforcement of Directive
2005/29/EC concerning unfair business-to-consumer commercial practices in
the internal market and Directive 2006/114/EC concerning misleading and
comparative advertising [2008/2114(INI)] - Committee on the Internal Market
and Consumer Protection. Rapporteur: Barbara Weiler (A6-0514/2008)

The following spoke: Barbara Weiler, who criticised the 'short
presentation' procedure provided for under Rule 131a, and made the
presentation of the report.

The following spoke: Androulla Vassiliou (Member of the Commission).

Following the remarks made by Barbara Weiler, the President recalled the
provisions of Rule 45(2).

The debate closed.

Vote: minutes of 13.01.2009, item 6.8.
