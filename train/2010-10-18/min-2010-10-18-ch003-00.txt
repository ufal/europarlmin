3. Announcements by the President

The President welcomed the award of the 2010 Nobel Peace Prize to the
Chinese dissident Liu Xiaobo. He called on the Chinese authorities to free
Liu Xiaobo and other human rights activists, and spoke of Parliament's
commitment to human rights.

The President added that the International Day for the Eradication of
Poverty had been celebrated on the previous day. Today, it was the fourth
European Anti-Trafficking Day. On Tuesday, Ban Ki-moon, Secretary-General
of the United Nations, would be visiting Parliament to commemorate the Day
for the Eradication of Poverty.

The President said that he would deliver his mid-term address on Wednesday
20 October 2010 (12.10-12.30).
