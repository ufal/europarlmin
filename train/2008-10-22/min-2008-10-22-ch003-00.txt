3. Protection of consumers in respect of certain aspects of timeshares ***I
(debate)

Report: on the proposal for a directive of the European Parliament and of
the Council on the protection of consumers in respect of certain aspects of
timeshare, long-term holiday products, resale and exchange [COM(2007)0303 -
C6-0159/2007 - 2007/0113(COD)] - Committee on the Internal Market and
Consumer Protection. Rapporteur: Toine Manders (A6-0195/2008)

Toine Manders introduced the report.

The following spoke: Meglena Kuneva (Member of the Commission).

The following spoke: Emanuel Jardim Fernandes (draftsman of the opinion of
the TRAN Committee), Antonio Lpez-Istriz White (draftsman of the opinion
of the JURI Committee), Malcolm Harbour, on behalf of the PPE-DE Group, and
Joel Hasse Ferreira, on behalf of the PSE Group.

                        IN THE CHAIR: Mechtild ROTHE
                               Vice-President

The following spoke: Heide Rhle, on behalf of the Verts/ALE Group, Leopold
Jzef Rutowicz, on behalf of the UEN Group, Andreas Schwab, Evelyne
Gebhardt, Charlotte Cederschild, Arlene McCarthy, Marian Zlotea and
Bernadette Vergnaud.

The following spoke under the 'catch-the-eye' procedure: Philip Bradbourn
and Zuzana Roithov.

The following spoke: Meglena Kuneva and Toine Manders.

The debate closed.
