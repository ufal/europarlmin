12. Order of business

The next item was the order of business.
The final draft agenda for the December 2009 sittings (PE 429.519/PDOJ) had
been distributed and a number of changes had been proposed (Rule 140):

Monday

No changes.
Tuesday

Request from the EFD Group for the inclusion of an oral question to the
Commission on the subsidiarity principle.

The following spoke: Francesco Enrico Speroni, on behalf of the EFD Group,
giving reasons for the request.

Parliament agreed to the request by electronic vote (118 in favour, 78
against, 1 abstention).

This item was included on the agenda for the sitting on Tuesday evening.

The tabling deadlines were established as follows:
motions for resolutions: Tuesday15 December 2009 at 12.00.

amendments and joint motion for a resolution: Wednesday, 16 December 2009
at 10.00.

The following spoke: Hannes Swoboda, on behalf of the S&D Group.

Wednesday

No changes.
Thursday

Debate on cases of breaches of human rights, democracy and the rule of law
(Rule 122):

Request from the EFD for the item on Azerbaijan  (item 49 on the final
draft agenda) to be replaced with an item on the Philippines.

The following spoke: Fiorello Provera, on behalf of the EFD Group, giving
reasons for the request.

Parliament rejected the request by roll-call vote (49 in favour, 156
against, 9 abstentions).

The order of business was thus established.
