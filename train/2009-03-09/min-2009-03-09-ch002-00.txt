2. Statements by the President

The President made a statement condemning, on behalf of Parliament, the
attack of 7 March 2009 on the Massereene army base in Northern Ireland, in
which two soldiers of the Royal Engineers - Mark Quinsey and Cengiz Azimkar
- had died, and several others had been wounded.

The House observed a minute's silence in memory of the victims.

The following spoke: James Nicholson, Liam Aylward and Jim Allister, who
associated themselves with the President's remarks.

                                      
                                      

The President made a statement on the occasion of International Women's
Rights Day 2009.
