3. Agenda

The President proposed the following change to the afternoon's agenda:

- the debate on the report on EUROPOL (Report: Agustn Daz de Mera Garca
Consuegra - A6-0447/2007) (item 34 on the agenda) would be brought forward
and would be held directly after the debate on the Council and Commission
statements on the situation in Kenya (item 55 on the agenda).

The following spoke: Hannes Swoboda, on behalf of the PSE Group, who spoke
in favour of the proposal on an exceptional basis.

The House agreed to the proposal.
The agenda was changed accordingly.
