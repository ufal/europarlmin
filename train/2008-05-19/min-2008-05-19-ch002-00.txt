2. Statement by the President

The President made a statement on Parliament's behalf condemning the attack
carried out by ETA on 14 May in Legutiano, in the Spanish Basque Country.
He conveyed Parliament's condolences to the family of the civil guard Juan
Manuel Piuel, who was killed in the attack, to the Spanish people and to
the democratic institutions of Spain.

Parliament observed a minute's silence in memory of the victim of the
attack.
