4. Vote on request for urgent procedure

Council request for the application of urgent procedure (Rule 134) for:

- EU-Mauritania fisheries agreement
* Proposal for a Council regulation on the conclusion of the Fisheries
Partnership Agreement between the European Community and the Islamic
Republic of Mauritania [COM(2006)0506 - C6-0334/2006 -  2006/0168(CNS)] -
PECH Committee
The following spoke: Rosa Migulez Ramos, on behalf of the PECH Committee.

The request for urgent procedure was approved.

The item was added to the agenda for the sitting of Thursday 16.11.2006.

The deadline for tabling amendments for plenary would be Wednesday
15.11.2006 at 10.00.
