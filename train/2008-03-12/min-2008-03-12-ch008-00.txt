8. Formal sitting - Celebration of the European Parliament's fiftieth
anniversary

From 15.10 to 16.15, a formal sitting of Parliament was held on the
occasion of the celebration of the European Parliament's fiftieth
anniversary.

         (The sitting was suspended at 16.15 and resumed at 16.20.)

                   IN THE CHAIR: Rodi KRATSA-TSAGAROPOULOU
                               Vice-President
