4. Calendar of part-sessions

The President announced that the Conference of Presidents had decided to
convene an extraordinary sitting, in accordance with Rule 134(3) of the
Rules of Procedure, on Wednesday, 23 June 2010 from 15.00 to 17.00. The
sitting would enable Herman Van Rompuy (President of the European Council)
to report to Parliament on the meeting of the European Council on 17 June
2010, in accordance with Article 15(6) of the Treaty on European Union.

The following spoke: Robert Atkins, on the distribution of documents in the
Chamber, and Werner Langen, who associated himself with those remarks. (The
President took note).
