2. Statements by the President

The President made a statement about the ceremonies commemorating the 70th
anniversary of the start of the Second World War.

                                      
                                      

He paid tribute to the memory of the two members of the Spanish Guardia
Civil murdered in the summer by ETA.

He paid tribute to the memory of Ernest Glinne and Christopher Prout,
former Members of the European Parliament, who had passed away on 10 August
2009 and 12 July 2009 respectively.

Parliament observed a minute's silence in honour of the deceased.
