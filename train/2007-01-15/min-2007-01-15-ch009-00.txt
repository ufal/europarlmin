9. Address by the President of Parliament

The President gave an address in which he took stock of his time as
President.

The following spoke: Joseph Daul, on behalf of the PPE-DE Group, Martin
Schulz, on behalf of the PSE Group, Graham Watson, on behalf of the ALDE
Group, Brian Crowley, on behalf of the UEN Group, Monica Frassoni, on
behalf of the Verts/ALE Group, Francis Wurtz, on behalf of the GUE/NGL
Group, Jens-Peter Bonde, on behalf of the IND/DEM Group, Bruno Gollnisch,
on behalf of the ITS Group, Irena Belohorsk, Ignasi Guardans Camb, Jean-
Claude Martinez (the President pointed out that because he had not spoken
in an official EU language his speech would not be recorded in the Verbatim
Report of Proceedings), and Margot Wallstrm (Vice-President of the
Commission).

The item closed.
