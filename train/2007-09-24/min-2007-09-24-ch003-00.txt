3. Approval of Minutes of previous sitting

The Minutes of the previous sitting were approved.

                                      
                                      

The following spoke: Philip Claeys, on behalf of the ITS Group, who stated
that his group would not participate in the election of the next winner of
the Sakharov Prize because the candidate proposed by the ITS Group had, he
said, been forced to withdraw his candidacy following pressure placed upon
him. (The President invited him to make his protest via other channels.)
