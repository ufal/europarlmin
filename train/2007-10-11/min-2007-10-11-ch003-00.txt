3. EC-USA air transport agreement * (debate)

Report on the proposal for a decision of the Council and the
Representatives of the Governments of the Member States of the European
Union, meeting with the Council on the conclusion of the Air Transport
Agreement between the European Community and its Member States, of the one
part, and the United States of America, of the other part [8044/3/07 -
COM(2006)0169 - C6 0210/2007 - 2006/0058(CNS)] - Committee on Transport and
Tourism. Rapporteur: Sad El Khadraoui (A6-0320/2007)

The following spoke: Jacques Barrot (Vice-President of the Commission).

Sad El Khadraoui introduced the report.

The following spoke: Reinhard Rack, on behalf of the PPE-DE Group, Brian
Simpson, on behalf of the PSE Group, Sarah Ludford, on behalf of the ALDE
Group, Eva Lichtenberger, on behalf of the Verts/ALE Group, Georgios
Toussas, on behalf of the GUE/NGL Group, Luca Romagnoli, on behalf of the
ITS Group, Georg Jarzembowski, Ulrich Stockmann, Georgios Georgiou, Jim
Higgins and Jacques Barrot.

The debate closed.

Vote: Minutes of 11.10.2007, Item 6.5.
