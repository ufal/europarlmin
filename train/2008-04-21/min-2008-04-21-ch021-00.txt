21. New Flame shipwreck and its consequences in Algeciras Bay (debate)

Commission statement: New Flame shipwreck and its consequences in Algeciras
Bay

Jacques Barrot (Vice-President of the Commission) made the statement.

The following spoke: Gerardo Galeote, on behalf of the PPE-DE Group, Luis
Yaez-Barnuevo Garca, on behalf of the PSE Group, Graham Watson, on behalf
of the ALDE Group, Ral Romeva i Rueda, on behalf of the Verts/ALE Group,
Willy Meyer Pleite, on behalf of the GUE/NGL Group, Lus Queir, Francisca
Pleguezuelos Aguilar, David Hammerstein, Luis de Grandes Pascual and Ins
Ayala Sender.

The following spoke: Jacques Barrot, Gerardo Galeote, who put a question to
Jacques Barrot, to which the latter responded, and Graham Watson on the two
preceding speakers' remarks.

As the motions for a resolution tabled were not yet available, they would
be announced in the House at a later time.

The debate closed.

Vote: minutes of 24.04.2008, item 7.3.
