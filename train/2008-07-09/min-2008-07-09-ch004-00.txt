4. Decision on urgent procedure

Council and Commission request for the application of urgent procedure
(Rule 134) for:

- * Proposal for a Council Regulation instituting a temporary specific
action aiming to promote the restructuring of the European Union fishing
fleets affected by the economic crisis [COM(2008)0454 - C6-0270/2008 -
2008/0144(CNS)] - PECH Committee

The following spoke: Philippe Morillon (Chairman of the PECH Committee).

The request for urgent procedure was approved.

This item was placed on the agenda of the day's sitting.

The deadline for tabling amendments for plenary would be Wednesday
09.07.2008 at 15.00.
