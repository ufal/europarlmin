2. Statements by the President

The President made statements on the Irish referendum of 2 October 2009,
welcoming the result, on the situation following the natural disaster in
Sicily, and on the occasion of the third anniversary of the murder of Anna
Politkovskaya.

Several Members held up banners bearing pictures of Anna Politkovskaya, and
the President associated himself with this tribute.

The following spoke: William (The Earl of) Dartmouth.
