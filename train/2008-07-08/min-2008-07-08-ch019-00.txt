19. Technical requirements and administrative procedures for commercial
transportation by aeroplane (EU OPS)/use of child-restraint safety devices
(OPS 1.730) (debate)

Oral question (O-0075/2008) by Eva Lichtenberger, on behalf of the TRAN
Committee, to the Commission: Technical requirements and administrative
procedures for commercial transportation by aeroplane (EU OPS)/use of child-
restraint safety devices (OPS 1.730) (B6-0169/2008).

Eva Lichtenberger moved the oral question.

Antonio Tajani (Vice-President of the Commission) answered the oral
question.

The following spoke: Georg Jarzembowski, on behalf of the PPE-DE Group,
Ulrich Stockmann, on behalf of the PSE Group, and Eva Lichtenberger, on
behalf of the Verts/ALE Group.

The following spoke under the 'catch-the-eye' procedure: Reinhard Rack and
Michael Cramer.

The following spoke: Antonio Tajani.

The debate closed.
