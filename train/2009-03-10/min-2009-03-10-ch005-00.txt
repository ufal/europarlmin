5. Type-approval requirements for the general safety of motor vehicles ***I
(debate)

Report: on the proposal for a regulation of the European Parliament and of
the Council concerning type-approval requirements for the general safety of
motor vehicles [COM(2008)0316 - C6-0210/2008 - 2008/0100(COD)] - Committee
on the Internal Market and Consumer Protection. Rapporteur: Andreas Schwab
(A6-0482/2008)

Andreas Schwab introduced the report.

The following spoke: Gnter Verheugen (Vice-President of the Commission).

The following spoke: Jorgo Chatzimarkakis (rapporteur for the opinion of
the ITRE Committee), Malcolm Harbour, on behalf of the PPE-DE Group, Gary
Titley, on behalf of the PSE Group, Olle Schmidt, on behalf of the ALDE
Group, Sen  Neachtain, on behalf of the UEN Group, Heide Rhle, on behalf
of the Verts/ALE Group, Jim Allister, Non-attached Member, Zuzana Roithov,
Wolfgang Bulfon and Toine Manders.

The following spoke under the 'catch-the-eye' procedure: Avril Doyle.

The following spoke: Gnter Verheugen and Andreas Schwab.

The debate closed.
