21. Fisheries Partnership Agreement between the EC and Denmark and
Greenland * (debate)

Report on the proposal for a Council regulation on the conclusion of the
Fisheries Partnership Agreement between the European Community on the one
hand, and the Government of Denmark and the Home Rule Government of
Greenland, on the other hand [COM(2006)0804 - C6-0506/2006 -
2006/0262(CNS)] - Committee on Fisheries. Rapporteur: Joop Post (A6-
0161/2007)

Joe Borg (Member of the Commission) spoke.

Joop Post introduced the report.

The following spoke: Helga Trpel (draftsman of the opinion of the BUDG
Committee), Carmen Fraga Estvez, on behalf of the PPE-DE Group, Rosa
Migulez Ramos, on behalf of the PSE Group, Elspeth Attwooll, on behalf of
the ALDE Group, Catherine Stihler and Joe Borg.

The debate closed.

Vote: Minutes of 22.05.2007, Item 9.7.
