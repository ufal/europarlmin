8. Fight against breast cancer in the European Union  (written declaration)

Written Declaration 0071/2009, submitted by Elizabeth Lynne, Michail
Tremopoulos, Lvia Jrka and Lidia Joanna Geringer de Oedenberg, on the
fight against breast cancer in the European Union, had been signed by the
majority of Parliament's component Members. Consequently, in accordance
with Rule 123(3) and (4), it would be forwarded to its addressees and
published, together with the names of the signatories, in the Texts Adopted
of the sitting of the following part-session with an indication in the
minutes of the names of the signatories. (See Annex 1 to the minutes of
5.5.2010.)

The following spoke: Elizabeth Lynne.
