4. Statement by the President

The President paid tribute to Angelo Vassallo, the Mayor of Pollica
(Italy), who had been murdered by the Camorra, the local mafia, on Sunday,
5 September 2010. The President reiterated Parliament's condemnation of all
forms of organised crime.

Parliament observed a minute's silence.
