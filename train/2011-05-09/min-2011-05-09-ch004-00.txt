4. Requests to defend parliamentary immunity

Luigi de Magistris presented two requests to defend his immunity and
privileges, in relation to pending court proceedings before the courts of
Cosenza and Milan.

Viktor Uspaskich presented a request to defend his immunity and privileges,
in relation to pending court proceedings before the court of Vilnius.

Corneliu Vadim Tudor presented a request to defend his immunity and
privileges.

In accordance with Rule 6(3), these requests were referred to the committee
responsible, the Committee on Legal Affairs.

The following spoke: Sergio Paolo Francesco Silvestris on Luigi de
Magistris's request.
