4. Decision on urgent procedure

Council request for the application of urgent procedure (Rule 142) for:

- the proposal for a Countil regulation amending Regulation (EC) No
1234/2007 establishing a common organisation of agricultural markets and on
specific provisions for certain agricultural products (Single CMO
Regulation) [COM(2009)0539 - C7-0223/2009 - 2009/0152(CNS)]

The following spoke: Paolo De Castro (Chair of the AGRI Committee), Martin
Husling and Albert De.

Parliament approved the request for urgent procedure.

This item was placed on the agenda for the sitting (Agenda for 20.10.2009,
item 46).

The deadline for tabling amendments for plenary would be 10.00 on
Wednesday, 21 October 2009.
