7. Approval of the minutes of the previous sitting

The minutes of the previous sitting were approved.

                                      
                                      

Marco Cappato asked for information on Members' activities  in  plenary  and
in committee to be published on Parliament's  website,  in  accordance  with
Parliament's position of 14 January 2009  (P6_TA(2009)0022)  (the  President
replied that the Bureau would discuss the matter at its next meeting).



Anna Zborsk, Chair of the FEMM committee, asked the President  to  express
Parliament's disapproval of the 'New Law on the  Afghan  Family'  which  had
recently been signed by Afghan President Hamid Karzai. She  also  asked  for
this point to be added to the agenda of  the  next  part-session  (this  was
noted by the President).
