1. Opening of sitting

The sitting opened at 9.05.

The following spoke: Hannes Swoboda, on behalf of the PSE Group, who,
referring to Parliament's decision to authorise the tabling, during the
current part-session, of motions for resolutions to wind up the debate on
the EU-Russia Summit(Minutes of 22.10.2007, Item 7), asked for the vote to
be taken during the next part-session, and Joseph Daul, on behalf of the
PPE-DE Group, who supported this request (the President replied that the
other political groups would be consulted).
