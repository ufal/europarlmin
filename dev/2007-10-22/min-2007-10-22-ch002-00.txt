2. Statement by the President

The President made a brief statement welcoming the outcome of the Lisbon
European Council (18 and 19 October 2007), which had seen the adoption of
the EU Reform Treaty, and he emphasised the importance of the Reform Treaty
for the European Parliament. He would proclaim the Charter of Fundamental
Rights at a formal sitting on 12 December 2007, the day before the Treaty
would be signed. He thanked Mr Brok, Mr Barn Crespo and Mr Duff,
Parliament's representatives at the IGC, for their work.

The following spoke: Daniel Cohn-Bendit and Hannes Swoboda.
