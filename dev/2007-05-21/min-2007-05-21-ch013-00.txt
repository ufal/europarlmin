13. Order of business

The next item was the order of business.
The final draft agenda for the May II and June I sittings (PE 389.455/PDOJ)
had been distributed and a number of changes had been proposed (Rule 132):

Sittings of 21.05.2007 to 24.05.2007

Monday
- no changes

Tuesday
Request from the Verts/ALE Group for the final vote on the report by Marie-
Hlne Aubert (A6-0061/2007) on organic production and labelling of organic
products (Item 16 on the Final Draft Agenda) to be held over until the
Strasbourg part-session in June.
The following spoke: Monica Frassoni, on behalf of the Verts/ALE Group, who
moved the request, and Struan Stevenson, on behalf of the PPE-DE Group.
Parliament rejected the request.
Request from the PPE-DE and PSE Groups for the debate on the Council and
Commission statements on the situation in Palestine (Item 62 on the Final
Draft Agenda) not to be wound up with motions for a resolution.

The following spoke: Hannes Swoboda, on behalf of the PSE Group, who moved
the request, and Francis Wurtz, on behalf of the GUE/NGL Group.
Parliament agreed to the request.

- no changes

Thursday
- no changes

Sittings of 06.06.2007 and 07.06.2007
