5. 2007 Sakharov Prize (announcement of winner)

The President announced that, at its meeting of the same day, the
Conference of Presidents had unanimously agreed to award the 2007 Sakharov
Prize to the Sudanese human rights lawyer Salih Mahmoud Osman.

The Prize would be presented on Tuesday 11 December in Strasbourg.

                                      
                                      

The President also announced that the Conference of Presidents had
unanimously agreed to pay tribute to the memory of the Russian journalist
Anna Politkovskaya at its next meeting.
