3. Statement by the President

The President made a statement on the murder of the Russian journalist Anna
Politkovskaya in Moscow on 7 October 2006. He pointed out that he had
called on the Russian authorities to do everything within their power to
find out who was responsible for the murder. He added that he had passed on
Parliament's condolences to Mrs Politkovskaya's family and friends.
Finally, the President pointed out that 63 journalists had been killed
across the world since the beginning of the year and that 120 journalists
were in prison.

Parliament observed a minute's silence.
