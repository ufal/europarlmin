5. Signing of REACH and the seventh framework research programme

Josep Borrell Fontelles (President of the European Parliament) and Matti
Vanhanen (President-in-Office of the Council) made short statements
following the adoption of the REACH programme and the seventh framework
research programme (Minutes of 13.12.2006, Item 8.1, Minutes of 13.12.2006,
Item 8.2, Minutes of 30.11.2006, Item 8.10, Minutes of 30.11.2006, Item
8.11, Minutes of 30.11.2006, Item 8.14, Minutes of 30.11.2006, Item 8.15,
Minutes of 30.11.2006, Item 8.16, Minutes of 30.11.2006, Item 8.17, Minutes
of 30.11.2006, Item 8.18, Minutes of 30.11.2006, Item 8.19, Minutes of
30.11.2006, Item 8.20 and Minutes of 30.11.2006, Item 8.21).
They then signed the acts, with Jos Manuel Barroso (President of the
Commission), Karl-Heinz Florenz (Chairman of the ENVI Committee), Guido
Sacconi (rapporteur on the REACH reports), Miloslav Ransdorf (Vice-Chairman
of the ITRE Committee) and the three rapporteurs on the seventh framework
research programme, Jerzy Buzek, Philippe Busquin and Anne Laperrouze.
